About ADHD (에드헤드)

Built for the way
some of us actually think.

Shop the Collection

ADHD (에드헤드) started with a simple observation: the fashion world was built for people who move in straight lines. We don't. So we built something for the ones who move sideways — fast, loud, and always mid-thought.

The name isn't a gimmick. Every founder, designer, and collaborator behind this brand carries the diagnosis or lives close to it. ADHD isn't our aesthetic — it's our operating system. The brand is an extension of that.

Each collection is released in limited drops, built around a single emotional state: Hyperfocus. Restlessness. Impulsivity. Scatter. We don't do seasons in the traditional sense. We do states of mind.
